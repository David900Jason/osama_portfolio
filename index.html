About Us

As the driving force behind The Script, we bring a unique blend of academic rigor and practical industry insight. Our profound expertise stems from specialized courses at prestigious institutions:

  • Screenwriting – Story & Structure (NYFA)
  • Screenwriting – Television Pilot (NYFA)
  • Professional Program in Writing for Television (UCLA)
  • Script Doctor – Script Reading (Script University)

We are the authors of 'Decoding the Script' (فك شفرة السيناريو), our Master's thesis - a seminal guide to the craft of screenwriting. Our extensive portfolio includes acclaimed films such as 'Rawi' (2023), 'Hepta: The Last Lecture' (2016), and 'The Mummy' (1999), among many other successful projects.

With years of experience in both academic and practical screenwriting, we offer comprehensive script analysis, development, and ghostwriting services that transform ideas into compelling, production-ready narratives.
