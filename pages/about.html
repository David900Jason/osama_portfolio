About us

Crafting compelling stories with over two decades of expertise in screenwriting and storytelling

Our Mission

At The Script, we believe that every story holds the power to move, inspire, and transform. Our mission is to empower visionaries—from aspiring writers to established production houses—to unlock the full potential of their narratives. We are dedicated to providing unparalleled expertise and bespoke solutions, breathing life into every script and ensuring it transcends the page to captivate the screen

Our Story

The Script was founded by Usama Hossam Eldin, a seasoned novelist and screenwriter with over two decades of invaluable experience. Usama's journey began in comedy, crafting sketches for various programs on Moga Comedy channel before moving into advertising, where he penned over 300 commercials for Egypt's leading companies.

Driven by an insatiable passion for storytelling, he refined his craft through an extensive academic pursuit in the United States, studying at prestigious institutions like the New York Film Academy (NYFA), Script University, and UCLA. This culminated in him earning a Master of Fine Arts (MFA) in Screenwriting from NYFA.
